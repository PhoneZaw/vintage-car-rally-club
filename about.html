Welcome to Vintage Car Company

About

Vintage Motorcar Co LLC was founded and incorporated by Paul Rose and John Bassler in 2001.

The early days of VMC were small but successful and in 2003, Paul and John opened a facility in Harpers Ferry, WV. The next two years brought about an expanding workload necessitating the addition of six employees and the purchase of land in Inwood, WV. In November 2006, Vintage Motorcar Company settled into its new 9000+ square foot facility with eight employees.

On January 1, 2018, John became sole proprietor of VMC. Presently VMC has twelve employees and continues to operate at the same level of meticulous professional service it has since its inception.
